BATCHES

We here offer four different types of Batches.

MEMBERS MORNING
6 - 8 A.M.

If you want to play matches with your friends and not really looking for training, this batch is for you. every morning from 6 to 8 am, Monday to Saturday 2 of the four courts are reserved for members. You can come and play matches with other members.


Fees-

2000/-


TRAINING MORNING
6 - 8 A.M.

While the members' batch consists of 2 1-1 hour batches and is happening on 2 of the courts there is also a training batch that is on going simultaneously on the other 2. This batch is for beginner and intermediate players. The intermediate players' training goes on on 1 court, and for the beginner players the training on the other 1 court. Even members who are newly starting tennis can join the beginner players and learn the basics. Manoj sir is the coach in-charge of the morning training.


Fees-

Beginner- 2000/-

Intermediate- 3000/-

PROFESSIONAL TRAINING
3 - 5 P.M.

This batch timing is reserved for professional player only. Only selected players are allowed at this time. Maximum 12 to 15 players play in this batch. The coach focuses on each one personally and designs the training drill according to the player's needs. The head coaches Tariq sir and Sumit sir personally focuses on the players along with the assistant coaches- Manoj sir, Himanshu sir and Mukul sir. The professional players are also taken to play in different academies to experience the different courts and play with different players.

After the batch there is also a daily workout scheduled of 45 minutes lead by Tariq sir himself. This is after the 2 hour training but is still included in the batch.


Fees-

2000/-


TRAINING EVENING
5 - 8 P.M.

The evening batches are for beginner and intermediate players. There are 3 different 1-1 hour batches for the players. All the beginner level batches are lead by Mukul sir and focuses on the overall development of the skills. The intermediate batch is lead by Himanshu sir and focues on individual players and their abilities and their lacking points.


Fees-

Beginner- 2000/-

Intermediate- 3000/-
